# Meaning and Teleology Baseline

## Core Restatement

UEOT's philosophical wager is that purpose and meaning need not be treated as
supernatural or purely subjective additions to nature. They can be interpreted
as dynamical relations internal to evolving objects.

## Domain Objects and Candidate Omega-Loops

The relevant objects range from living agents to cognitive selves and
civilizational formations. Their `Omega`-loop structures are what make a stable
trajectory available in the first place.

## Pi/Phi Translation

- `Pi`: immediate viability, payoff, satisfaction, or order-preserving action
- `Phi`: understanding, coherence, uncertainty reduction, future-sensitive
  structure

## GOD / GOA

Purpose is best understood as relation to `GOA`, while lived direction or
practical orientation is best understood through `GOD`.

## Lifecycle Interpretation

Meaning is not static. It changes with emergence, maturation, crisis, and
reorganization. Teleological language must therefore remain lifecycle-aware.

## Testable Consequence

If UEOT cannot distinguish meaningful directedness from any ordinary
optimization process, its philosophical contribution collapses into triviality.

## Cross-Domain Links

- Mind science supplies the strongest bridge between control, self, and lived
  directedness.
- Socioeconomics tests whether teleological language scales beyond individuals
  without turning into ideology.
