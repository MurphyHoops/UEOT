# Mind Baseline

## Core Restatement

UEOT treats mind as an organized, nested, actively maintained loop hierarchy
rather than an exception to nature. The ambition is to explain agency and
control without invoking supernatural purpose.

## Domain Objects and Candidate Omega-Loops

The object is the integrated mind or self instantiated by recurrent neural and
cognitive organization. Candidate `Omega`-loops span neural recurrence,
attention-control loops, and multiscale integration that preserves an enduring
point of view.

## Pi/Phi Translation

- `Pi`: metabolic economy, behavioral payoff, action efficiency
- `Phi`: uncertainty reduction, learning, model refinement, attentional
  coherence

## GOD / GOA

- `GOD`: local cognitive-behavioral direction that improves coherent adaptive
  control
- `GOA`: a more stable and integrated regime of self-maintaining cognition

## Lifecycle Interpretation

Mind develops from immature openness toward more selective control, then may
destabilize under pathology, exhaustion, or degeneration.

## Testable Consequence

UEOT should predict where integration breakdown interrupts the self-maintaining
loop architecture underlying conscious control.

## Cross-Domain Links

- Biology grounds the living substrate required for cognitive persistence.
- Meaning/philosophy must translate cognitive directedness into a disciplined
  account of value and purpose.
