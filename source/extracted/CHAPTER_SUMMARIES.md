# Chapter Summaries

## Chapter 1: The Quest for a Unified Science

Frames the fracture between law-governed matter and purpose-laden life and
mind. Positions UEOT relative to systems theory, cybernetics, information
physics, autopoiesis, and related integrative traditions.

## Chapter 2: Core Ontology and Dynamical Laws

Defines UEOT objects through `Omega`-loops, introduces loop complexity,
openness, dynamical modes, and establishes the `Pi/Phi` principle alongside
`GOD` and `GOA`.

## Chapter 3: Explanatory Power Through Case Studies

Turns UEOT into a cross-domain diagnostic program spanning biological
evolution, mind dynamics, socioeconomic systems, and the problem of meaning,
value, and will.

## Chapter 4: Open Research Program

Extends UEOT toward mathematical formalization, quantum mechanics, particle
topology, emergent gravity, cosmology, and explicit experimental proposals.

## Conclusion

Recasts UEOT as both a scientific and worldview-level proposal, emphasizing
falsifiability, openness, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.
