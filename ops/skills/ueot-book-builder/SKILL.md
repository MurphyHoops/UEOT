# UEOT Book Builder

Use this skill when writing or updating `book/chapters/`.

## Rules

- Every chapter claim must map back to accepted memos or architect resolutions.
- Keep unresolved gaps visible.
- Do not present speculative frontier claims as settled doctrine.

## Minimum Inputs

- target chapter dossier
- supporting memos
- relevant architect resolutions
